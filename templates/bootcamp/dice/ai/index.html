Production Notes (FAQ)

Do I need professional equipment to start?
No. Our beginner tracks are designed to teach composition and storytelling using whatever camera you have available, including smartphones.
Is the curriculum self-paced?
Yes. While we have scheduled live critiques, all core modules are available on-demand so you can learn around your production schedule.
How does the portfolio review work?
At the end of every semester, your final project is reviewed by a panel of industry experts who provide personalized video feedback.
Can I collaborate with other students?
Absolutely. Our "Studio Hub" allows you to find writers, editors, and sound engineers within the community to build your crew.
Is financial aid available?
We offer scholarships for underrepresented voices in film and flexible payment plans for all students. Contact admissions for details.
