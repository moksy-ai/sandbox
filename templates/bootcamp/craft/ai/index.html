Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding skills to use Craft?

Not at all. Our platform is completely drag-and-drop. You can build landing pages, courses, and checkout flows in minutes without writing a single line of code.

How do I get paid?

We integrate directly with Stripe and PayPal. Payments from your customers go straight to your bank account instantly. We don't hold your funds.

Can I use my own custom domain?

Yes! Every plan includes the ability to connect your own custom domain (e.g., www.yourname.com) so your brand stays front and center.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans. You can explore all features, build your site, and launch your product before you pay a dime.

Can I migrate my email list?

Absolutely. We provide easy import tools for CSV files, or you can sync directly from Mailchimp, ConvertKit, and other major providers.
