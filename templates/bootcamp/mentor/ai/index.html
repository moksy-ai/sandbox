Design Fundamentals & Research

Start your journey by mastering the core principles of visual design and user psychology. You will learn how to conduct effective user research, create empathy maps, and define user personas to ensure your designs solve real human problems.

  • Design Thinking Process
  • User Research & Personas
  • Information Architecture

Wireframing & Prototyping

Translate your research into functional blueprints. This module focuses on low-fidelity wireframing and interactive prototyping using Figma. You'll learn how to map out user flows and test functionality before moving to high-fidelity visuals.

  • Low-Fidelity Wireframes
  • Interactive Figma Prototypes
  • Usability Testing Basics

Visual Interface Design

Bring your designs to life with advanced UI techniques. Master color theory, typography, and grid systems. You'll learn how to create visually stunning interfaces that are accessible, modern, and aligned with current industry design trends.

  • Typography & Color Science
  • Grid Systems & Layouts
  • Iconography & UI Kits

Design Systems & Handoff

Learn how to design at scale by building comprehensive design systems. We cover component-based architecture and the essential developer handoff process to ensure your creative vision is implemented perfectly by engineering teams.

  • Reusable Component Libraries
  • Developer Handoff via Zeplin/Figma
  • Documentation Best Practices

Advanced User Experience

Take a deep dive into complex UX challenges. This month focuses on accessibility standards (WCAG), emotional design, and data-driven iteration. You will learn how to analyze user behavior data to make informed design improvements.

  • Accessibility & Inclusive Design
  • Heuristic Evaluation
  • A/B Testing Strategies

Capstone Project

Apply everything you've learned to a massive real-world project. You'll take a product from initial research through to a high-fidelity, tested prototype. This project serves as the crown jewel of your professional design portfolio.

  • End-to-End Case Study
  • User Testing & Iteration
  • Final Presentation Prep

Portfolio & Career Prep

Prepare for the job market with dedicated career support. We help you build a stunning online portfolio, polish your LinkedIn profile, and conduct mock interviews to ensure you can confidently articulate your design process to hiring managers.

  • Portfolio Website Building
  • Resume & LinkedIn Optimization
  • Mock Technical Interviews

Graduation & Networking

Celebrate your success and join our exclusive alumni network. Gain access to our job board, industry networking events, and lifelong learning resources. You'll leave the bootcamp not just with a certificate, but with a community of designers.

  • Final Portfolio Review
  • Industry Certificate
  • Alumni Job Board Access
