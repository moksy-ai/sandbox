What You'll Learn

Our curriculum is designed specifically for beginners. We skip the confusing jargon and focus on the practical, modern skills you actually need to build real apps and get hired in tech.

Module 1: Web Basics

Start your journey by mastering the building blocks of the web. You'll learn HTML to structure pages, CSS to make them beautiful, and basic JavaScript to add simple interactivity.

Module 2: Interactive UIs

Bring your websites to life! Learn how to use modern, industry-standard tools like React.js to build fast, interactive user interfaces that look amazing on any screen size.

Module 3: UX/UI Principles

A great app isn't just functional; it has to be intuitive. Discover how to design with the user in mind, creating layouts and experiences that people actually love to use.

Module 4: Back-End Basics

Look under the hood and see how the web really works. Understand how data is stored in databases and learn to build simple APIs to power your front-end applications.

Module 5: Real-World Projects

Put everything you've learned together. Work on collaborative coding projects that simulate a real work environment, giving you the perfect pieces to show off in your portfolio.

Module 6: Career Prep

We don't just teach you to code; we teach you how to get hired. Practice mock interviews, polish your tech resume, and connect with our network of hiring partners.

Common Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

Not at all! Our curriculum is built from the ground up for complete beginners. If you know how to browse the internet, you're ready to start learning with us.

How much time do I need to commit?

Our program is highly flexible. Most of our successful students dedicate about 10-15 hours a week, making it easy to balance with a full-time job or family commitments.

What happens if I get stuck on a project?

You are never alone. You'll have unlimited access to our 1-on-1 mentorship sessions and a 24/7 Slack community of peers and instructors ready to help you untangle any code.

Will you actually help me find a job?

Absolutely. Our dedicated career coaches will help you build a stunning portfolio, write a tech-focused resume, and run mock interviews until you land your first role.

Do you offer flexible payment plans?

Yes we do! We believe education should be accessible. We offer upfront discounts, monthly installments, and Income Share Agreements so you don't pay until you get hired.
